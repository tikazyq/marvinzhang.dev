Figure 02 — Two new selection criteria

The loop an agent runs, and what your stack controls

Per task the loop closes dozens to hundreds of times. The stack sets its latency; the stack decides how much correctness is machine-decidable at the branch.
Dense signal: a tsc error is free, deterministic, machine-readable — same defect, same message, same line. 94% of compiler errors in LLM output are type-check failures.
Sparse signal: a production TypeError is expensive to reach, stochastic to reproduce, and needs a human to interpret.
