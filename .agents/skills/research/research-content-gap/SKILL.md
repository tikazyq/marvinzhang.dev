# Content Gap Research

Validate topic uniqueness and find differentiation angles before writing.

## When to Use

- Before starting any new article — "Is this worth writing?"
- When a topic already has extensive coverage elsewhere
- To find a unique angle that justifies the effort
- To understand what existing content misses

## Research Process

### 1. Survey Existing Content

Search for 5-10 existing articles on the same topic:
- Technical blogs (dev.to, Medium, personal blogs)
- Official documentation and guides
- Conference talks and videos
- Books and courses

### 2. Map the Coverage

For each piece of existing content, document:

| Article | Author/Source | Angle | Depth | Audience | Gap |
|---------|-------------|-------|-------|----------|-----|
| [Title] | [Source]    | [Angle] | Shallow/Medium/Deep | [Who it serves] | [What it misses] |

### 3. Identify Gaps

Common gaps to look for:
- **Depth gap**: Existing content is too shallow or too academic
- **Audience gap**: No content for your specific audience level
- **Freshness gap**: Existing content is outdated
- **Perspective gap**: Missing a practitioner/theoretical/comparative view
- **Language gap**: No quality Chinese content on this topic
- **Integration gap**: Topic covered in isolation, not connected to broader ecosystem

### 4. Define Your Unique Value Proposition

Answer:
- **What's different?** How is your take distinct from what exists?
- **Why you?** What unique perspective or experience do you bring?
- **Why now?** What makes this timely?
- **For whom?** Who specifically will benefit most?

## Deliverables

```markdown
# Content Gap Analysis: [Topic]

## Existing Coverage (Top 5)
| # | Title | Source | Angle | Gap |
|---|-------|--------|-------|-----|

## Identified Gaps
1. [Gap description]
2. [Gap description]

## Unique Value Proposition
**Our angle**: [What makes this article different]
**Target audience**: [Who benefits]
**Timing**: [Why now]

## Recommendation
[Write / Skip / Adjust angle]
```

## Quality Gates

- [ ] Reviewed 5+ existing articles on the topic
- [ ] Gaps clearly articulated (not just "we haven't written about it")
- [ ] Unique value proposition is specific and defensible
- [ ] Target audience identified
- [ ] Clear recommendation: write, skip, or adjust angle
